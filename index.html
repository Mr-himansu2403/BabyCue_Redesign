Our Mission

Pediatric healthcare
reimagined with precision AI

Every year, diarrheal diseases claim nearly 500,000 lives under age five — not from a lack of treatment, but from delayed, incorrect diagnosis. BabyCue changes this equation fundamentally.

Founded in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, BabyCue combines lateral flow diagnostics with AI-powered gut health analytics — bridging the gap between first symptom and correct care for children in clinics, communities, and low-resource settings.

Product 01 · Diagnostics

DiaCue — Rapid Diarrhea
Diagnostic Kit

The first rapid diagnostic kit designed to differentiate bacterial from non-bacterial diarrhea in children — delivering point-of-care clarity in just 20 minutes, without a laboratory.

How It Works
  • 1
    Stool Sample Collection
    Minimal sample volume required. Simple, standardized collection process suitable for all clinical settings.
  • 2
    Lateral Flow Immunoassay
    Proprietary antigen-based detection technology identifies bacterial pathogens with high precision.
  • 3
    Read & Interpret Results
    Clear visual readout in 20 minutes. Scan with GI-BUD app for AI-assisted interpretation and care pathway.
  • 4
    Targeted Treatment Decision
    Prescribe antibiotics only when bacterial infection is confirmed — reducing AMR risk significantly.

Product 02 · AI Companion

Meet GI-BUD

An AI-powered gut health companion that transforms raw DiaCue data into intelligent, personalized care guidance for every child.

  • 📸
    DiaCue Result Scanner
    Point your phone at the DiaCue strip. AI reads, interprets, and logs results instantly — no manual data entry.
  • 👅
    AI Tongue Hydration Analysis
    Proprietary computer vision model assesses dehydration severity from a simple tongue photograph.
  • 📊
    Personalized Gut Health Score
    A dynamic, child-specific score synthesizing diagnostic data, hydration, diet, and historical patterns.
