Our Story

Our Story

BabyCue was born from a simple yet powerful observation: in resource-limited healthcare settings across India, pediatricians often prescribe antibiotics empirically due to lack of rapid, affordable diagnostic tools. This practice contributes to antimicrobial resistance (AMR) — one of the most pressing global health threats.

Founded in 2022 in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, our founding team combined expertise in AI, medical diagnostics, and public health to create a solution that bridges the diagnostic gap. Today, BabyCue is recognized nationally and internationally for its innovative approach to pediatric gut health and AMR reduction.
