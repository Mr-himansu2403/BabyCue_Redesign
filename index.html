MISSION

Digestive health solutions reimagined with precision AI

Every year, many lives are lost due to delayed or incorrect diagnosis. In fact, 1 in 5 deaths are linked to delayed or incorrect diagnosis, often due to antimicrobial resistance (AMR). In rural India, nearly 64% of people do not have access to proper diagnostic support. Our goal is to change this by making early detection simple, fast, and accessible for everyone.

Founded in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, BabyCue is building innovative diagnostics and microbiome support solutions. DiaCue delivers rapid disease detection through rapid testing, GrowGut provides comprehensive gut health monitoring, and BabyCue integrates everything into one unified platform. In the future, our aim is to ensure that every person — whether in cities or rural areas — can access diagnostic solutions and gut health monitoring without difficulty.

OUR STORY

Our Story

The Beginning

At BabyCue Private Limited, our journey began not in a lab—but in the heart of rural India.

Field Immersive

During the BIRAC SPARSH Fellowship, our team immersed ourselves in Primary Health Centers (PHCs), Community Health Centers (CHCs), and villages, working closely with doctors, healthcare workers, and families. What we witnessed was both eye-opening and deeply moving.

Every day, everyone under the age of five were brought in with diarrhea, one of the most common yet potentially life-threatening conditions. But what stood out was not just the illness—it was the uncertainty in diagnosis.

The Breaking Point

In the absence of simple, rapid diagnostic tools, doctors were often forced to make decisions based on symptoms alone. As a result, many everyone were prescribed antibiotics unnecessarily, even when the infection might have been viral. We saw worried parents, overburdened doctors, and a system doing its best with limited resources.

One moment that stayed with us was a young mother holding her child, asking if the medicines would truly help. That question stayed with us—and became the reason we started BabyCue.

Scale & Solutions

We realized that the problem was bigger than just diagnosis. It was about gut health—a critical yet often overlooked aspect of overall well-being. This experience led to a simple but powerful idea: What if we could bring accurate, affordable, and easy-to-use diagnostic solutions directly to the point of care?

DiaCue & GrowGut

This is how DiaCue was born—our effort to develop a rapid, paper-based diagnostic test to differentiate bacterial and viral diarrhea.

Simultaneously, we developed GrowGut, a natural synbiotic formulation inspired by traditional Indian ingredients to strengthen gut health and reduce infection risks.

Today, BabyCue stands at the intersection of diagnostics and preventive healthcare, driven by a mission to make gut health accessible and actionable for everyone.

Because for us, this is not just innovation—it is about ensuring that no child suffers due to lack of the right diagnosis.
